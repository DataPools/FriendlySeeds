Make Minecraft Seeds Friendlier!

Convert numerical Minecraft seeds to words & letters.

What is this?

If you have a long numerical Minecraft (Java or Bedrock Edition) seed that want to easily share with your friends, this tool is for you!

Just input your seed above, and it will get converted to letters.

Note: Due to the constraints of Java, only seeds between -2,147,483,648 and 2,147,483,647 can be converted to letters.
